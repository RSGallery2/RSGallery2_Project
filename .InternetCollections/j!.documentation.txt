 users documentation
 https://guide.joomla.org
 

You can easily make a new article by 
logging in with your Joomla Identity Portal 

* login here: https://guide.joomla.org/contributors. 
* You'll then see a "Draft Articles" menu-item where you can just create a new article. 

Herman Peeren will then look after it that the things you edit will be published in that article. 
Another possibility is to just add an article with only the changes, 
or maybe with the list of icons, if you don't want to change anything in the rest of the articles. 

Update Articles
The easiest way is to just copy the existing article and past it into a new article. 
The someone will care for it

Become a Knowledge Navigator: Contributions to the new Joomla! documentation are very welcome.
