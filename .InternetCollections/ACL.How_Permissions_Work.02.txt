Dear extension devs,

in the light of recent events:

CSRF (checkToken) is not ACL, you always have to implement your own ACL checks, especially in custom controller endpoints https://manual.joomla.org/docs/next/security/csrf-protection/
com_ajax has no built-in logic for ACL, it's completly on your end to implement proper ACL checks: https://manual.joomla.org/docs/next/building-extensions/components/component-examples/ajaxdemo/#ajax-controller
if you are re-using backend controllers in the frontend, it's even more important to have proper ACL checks in place, as the generic "user can at least log in into the admin area"-type of check from the applcation level is lost
Thank you!
